Our Story

Luxury Estates is #1 in the Indiana MLS for transactions with over $2 billion in sales. For nearly two decades, Luxury Estates has been the top-producing real estate company in Indiana. With over 30 years of experience and closing over $2 billion in sales in the Indiana real estate markets, Luxury Estates continually rank in the top 1% of agents worldwide. Our team has exceptional knowledge about Indiana communities and neighborhoods. Our ability to market and showcase your property is optimized with the most innovative technologies and resources available in today's market.
